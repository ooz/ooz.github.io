About

Hey! I am Oliver, a Software Engineer at ePages. When not sitting infront of computers I like doing various sports (Shotokan Karate, BWE, Yoga, running, swimming etc.), messing around with Lego bricks and playing the guitar ukulele.

What's the deal with the logo/avatar?

It's a modified version of the German Naturschutzgebiet (nature reserve) sign. Since the original image is in the public domain, I could crop it to look like a selfie and add the letter z in succession of the oo owl eyes to match my GitHub handle ooz.
